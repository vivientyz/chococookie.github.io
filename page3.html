How to get there

Tampines Eco Green is an eco-friendly park that offers a sanctuary for flora and fauna and a place for nature recreation.

How to get there:
Alight at Tampines MRT station and walk 20 mins to the park via Sun Plaza Park

Park size: 36 hectares

Park lighting hours: No lights after dark

Accessibility info: Wheelchair accessible

Specific Dos and Don't: No pets allowed, no cycling, no poaching, no mass gathering and no fishing

Tips for a safe and enjoyable trip

✔ Dress comfortably and wear suitable footwear.
✔ Park your bicycle at the stands provided at the park entrance as cycling is not allowed in the park.
✔ Walk along the designated paths to protect the natural environment of the park.
✔ Do not feed the animals.
✔ Do your part to bring rubbish out of the park.
✔ Activities such as fishing, poaching, releasing of animals and those that cause pollution are strictly prohibited.
✔ Camping is not allowed.
